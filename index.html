NBA


The NBA is a basketball organization for talented atheletes. Nba stands for National Basketball Association. The NBA has been around for years, it was founded june 6, 1946 in new york.Each year they do a draft to get new players on each team. There are a total of 30 teams in the NBA.


NBA rules





The NBA has many rules like how you are not allowed to carry the ball and many other fouls like: charging fouls, blocking fouls, shooting fouls, reach-in fouls, technical fouls and even more. Shooting fouls are one of the most common fouls in the NBA. When someone commits a shooting foul on someone the that person gets to take two free throws.
